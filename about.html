About Us

James Exports is a division of Excellent Colour Printers that engages in a variety of commercial endeavors. It has been manufacturing safety matches in Kovilpatti since 2010Â and supplies the home market, which has a high demand. Later in 2022, it began its journey into exports to meet the demands of the international market by employing imported equipment in the process of producing the highest quality match boxes. For the best production, our factory is entirely automated. For the greatest finish quality and to shorten the time between order placing and delivery, we employ automated technology that is integrated. Splints, boards, and chemicals are just a few examples of the high-quality raw materials we employ to create our own distinctive manufacturing processes. In Tamilnadu, India, we are now a leading producer and exporter. Long-term relationships over big profits are our company's guiding principle.
